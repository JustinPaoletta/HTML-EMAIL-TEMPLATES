Start Here


WARNING! The email preview will render images in a slightly different position than what they will be once the email is sent. What you see in the browser is what it should look like once the email is sent. Best practice would be to continue to test what it looks like until you get it just right because of these differences. Additionally the original size of the images matter, you will need to keep them small to fit inside the table cells.

How To Test


To send the file via Apple Mail, first have the file open in Safari. Then all you need to do is click the share icon and choose "Email This Page". Within the mail app make sure it says "Send Web Content As: Web Page" on the top right hand side. Keep in mind your images may not look like they are in the correct position at this point. Send the email to yourself and you will see it is corrected once sent.
